The goal is not to write a long reflection.
The goal is to extract reusable lessons fast.

This document helps decide:
- whether the game idea has promise
- what slowed the workflow down
- what Claude handled well or badly
- what should be added to the template system
- whether the prototype should be continued, archived, or dropped
