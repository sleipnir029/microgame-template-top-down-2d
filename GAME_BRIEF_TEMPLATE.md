# EXAMPLE

## Title
Orbit Panic

## One-line pitch
Survive in a small arena while dodging incoming hazards and collecting points.

## Core fantasy
You are a tiny ship barely surviving in a dangerous neon arena.

## Core loop
Move, dodge, collect, survive longer, retry after death.

## Controls
WASD or arrow keys only.

## Goal
Score as high as possible by surviving and collecting pickups.

## Lose condition
Touch any hazard.

## Session target
20–60 seconds.

## Required entities
Player, hazard, pickup, HUD.

## Camera / view
Top-down flat 2D.

## Visual vibe
Minimal neon arcade.

## Juice priorities
Pickup burst, hit flash, small camera shake, punchy death pause.

## Out of scope
No upgrades, no multiple enemy types, no menus beyond title/game over, no story.

## Success test
A player understands the loop in 5 seconds and wants to immediately retry after losing.
